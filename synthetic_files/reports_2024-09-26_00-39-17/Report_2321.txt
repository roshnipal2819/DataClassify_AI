### Detailed Descriptions and Interpretations



The interpretation of the measurements involved complex interactions due to the varying nature of each mixture. Significant findings include:



- **Viscosity Analysis:** The Viscometer detached subtle differences between Mixture E and F. The readings of 2596.03 cP and 5166.67 cP suggest distinct textural qualities, which are instrumental in product formulation strategies in the cosmetic industry.

  

- **Diffraction and Thermal Stability:** Using the X-Ray Diffractometer, the thermal stability of Mixture C was highlighted at 120.5°C, indicating its potential in high-temperature applications. Unexpectedly, the Thermocycler evidenced Mixture B’s integrity up to 78.5°C only.



Predictably, the soothing properties associated with Vitamin E became evident via the Ion Chromatograph, where Mixture F presented a discernible peak at 55.3 mM, implying enhanced antioxidant capabilities.
