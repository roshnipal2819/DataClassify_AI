### Observational Insights



The analysis of materials went beyond quantitative measurements; each test instrument revealed nuanced characteristics of the mixtures. Observations provided qualitative insights, not just numerical.



**Observations:**



- **Coconut Oil mixtures** tend to exhibit high stability in viscosity tests, indicating a robust molecular structure.

- **Almond Oil** mixtures displayed unique conductivity properties, suggesting potential for use in electrochemically sensitive applications.

- The inclusion of **Vitamin E** in mixtures notably enhanced rheological properties, particularly in terms of flow behavior.

- **Beeswax** presence was correlated with increased crystalline structure, as noted in the X-Ray analysis.



*Note: Anomalies were observed, possibly due to equipment calibration, though this remains uncertain.*

#### Complex Description of Processes



The High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) process involved delicate calibration of solvent gradients to optimize separation. Conductivity was assessed using a finely tuned electrode within the CM-215, ensuring accurate ionic interaction measurements. Rheological properties required precise control of shear rate and temperature to emulate realistic conditions. X-Ray diffraction patterns were indexed via advanced software algorithms to elucidate crystallinity.
