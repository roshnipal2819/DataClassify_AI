## Introduction

## Materials and Methods

- **Instrument:** HPLC System HPLC-9000

- **Sample:** Coconut Oil, Gum, Glycerin



The sample mixture comprising coconut oil, gum, and glycerin was analyzed to quantify the concentration of active components in the mixture.

## Interpretation of Results  



The data obtained from the HPLC, NMR, and GC illustrate the diverse concentration profiles of compounds within the samples. Rheological characteristics indicate the complex nature of these formulations, potentially impacting their practical applications. Each method provided unique insights into the oil mixtures' physical and chemical properties that are essential for industrial optimizations.



- **Key Insight:** Jojoba oil mixtures show potential as superior lubricants due to their measured viscosities and superior stability under thermal conditions.
