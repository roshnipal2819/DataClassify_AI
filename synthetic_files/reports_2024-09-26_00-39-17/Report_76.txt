## Abstract:

The objective of Report_76 was to analyze various mixtures composed of oils and additives using multiple analytical techniques. These mixtures were investigated for their compositional integrity, physical properties, and structural characteristics. Complex matrices involved in the study included combinations of Coconut Oil, Jojoba Oil, Almond Oil, and several additives such as Gum, Glycerin, Vitamin E, and others. A thorough analysis provided insights into the role each component plays within the matrix.



## Materials and Methods:



### Sample Preparation:

Each mixture was meticulously prepared by combining specified ingredients. Each combination was considered a separate test sample. Mixtures were prepared by integrating precise volumes and concentrations of oils and additives.
