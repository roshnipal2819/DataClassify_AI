# Lab Report 465: Analysis of Various Oil Mixtures



## Introduction



This report details the analysis of various samples involving oil mixtures. The aim was to measure different properties using advanced instrumentation. The samples included combinations of Jojoba Oil, Almond Oil, and various other components such as Beeswax, Glycerin, and Vitamin E, among others. Each sample was tested using different systems to evaluate specific parameters.



---



## Sample Composition Summary



Besides the main oils, each sample contained unique components for analysis:



- **Sample A**: Jojoba Oil, Glycerin (Conductivity, pH, and NMR analysis).

- **Sample B**: Almond Oil, Gum, Vitamin E (X-ray diffraction).

- **Sample C**: Jojoba Oil, Beeswax, Glycerin (HPLC analysis).

- **Sample D**: Almond Oil (Optical density).

- **Sample E**: Almond Oil, Cetyl Alcohol (Liquid chromatography and PCR).



The tests aimed to understand each mixture's properties, contributing to their potential applications in various fields.



---



## Measurements and Observations



### Sample A: Conductivity and Chemical Characteristics



1. **Conductivity Meter CM-215**  

   **Result**: 1450 µS/cm  

   The sample containing Jojoba Oil and Glycerin exhibited significant ionic conduction, suitable for potential electrochemical applications.



2. **pH Meter PH-700**  

   **Result**: 7 pH  

   A neutral pH indicates compatibility with a variety of biological systems.



3. **NMR Spectrometer NMR-500**  

   **Result**: 10 ppm  

   The spectra revealed distinct signals corresponding to chemical shifts unique to the mixture's composition.



### Sample B: Structural Analysis



- **X-Ray Diffractometer XRD-6000**  

  **Result**: 120°C  

  The crystalline structure analysis indicates robust stability at elevated temperatures, useful for thermal applications.



### Sample C: Concentration and Optical Properties



- **HPLC System HPLC-9000**  

  **Result**: 500 mg/L  

  The high concentration of active components provides effectiveness for targeted applications.



### Sample D: Optical Density



- **Microplate Reader MRX**  

  **Result**: 3.0 OD  

  Such optical density suggests potential for absorptive or reflective applications in optical devices.
