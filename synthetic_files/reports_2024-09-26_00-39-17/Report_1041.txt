## Equipment and Methods



### Instruments Utilized:



1. **Four Ball Tester FB-1000**: Evaluates wear preventive characteristics.

2. **Rheometer R-4500**: Measures the viscosity and flow characteristics.

3. **Ion Chromatograph IC-2100**: Determines ionic concentrations.

4. **Thermocycler TC-5000**: Used for precise temperature control.

5. **Centrifuge X100**: Applies centrifugal force to separate components.

6. **Viscometer VS-300**: Measures the viscosity in centipoise (cP).

- Jojoba Oil combinations

- Coconut Oil combinations

- Almond Oil combinations
