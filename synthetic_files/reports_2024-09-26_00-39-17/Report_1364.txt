# Lab Report: Characterization of Cosmetic Formulations



## Introduction



This report presents the analytical characterization of various cosmetic formulations using different analytical techniques. The study focuses on mixtures primarily containing Almond Oil, Jojoba Oil, and Coconut Oil, each combined with diverse additives such as Gum, Beeswax, Cetyl Alcohol, Glycerin, and Vitamin E. Various sophisticated instruments were employed to determine physical and chemical properties that influence the cosmetic applications of these blends.



## Instruments and Methods



1. **X-Ray Diffractometer XRD-6000**  

   Utilized to gauge crystalline structures, primarily beneficial for assessing the amalgamation of Almond Oil with Gum and Glycerin under mild thermal conditions.



2. **FTIR Spectrometer FTIR-8400**  

   Employed to ascertain functional groups in formulations comprising Almond Oil, Gum, and Vitamin E, detecting structural bonds responsible for their intended cosmetic properties.



3. **HPLC System HPLC-9000**  

   Critical for measuring the concentration of bioactive compounds within samples like Almond Oil and Beeswax.



4. **Thermocycler TC-5000**  

   Applied for thermal cycling, particularly suitable for mixtures containing Jojoba Oil, Beeswax, and Glycerin.



5. **Conductivity Meter CM-215**  

   Used to evaluate the ionic conductivity of solutions, particularly involving Almond Oil combined with Cetyl Alcohol and Vitamin E.



6. **Viscometer VS-300**  

   Essential for assessing the viscosity profiles of several combinations under study, providing insights into the textural feel of final formulations.

### Additional Results

- FTIR analysis revealed unique vibrational signatures, indicative of the reactive sites within molecules when Almond Oil meets Gum and Vitamin E.

- HPLC runs underscored the precision in quantifying relevant nutritive elements in the oils, with Almond Oil and Beeswax mixtures revealing optimal metric values.

- Jojoba Oil synergistically interacting with Beeswax illustrates stable temperature resistance, essential for controlled cosmetic settings.
